  • Question: Who organizes Angular U

    Answer: Angular U is a Silicon Valley Code Camp production that includes Peter Kellner. Dan Wahlin leads efforts around Angular Content and Kevin Nilson is responsible for other content.

  • Question: Can I attend the workshops if I am not attending the main Monday and Tuesday conference?

    Answer: If there is space available in our workshops after our conference fills up we will open up spaces to those who are not attending the main conference.

  • Question: Once tickets sell out is there any chance I can still get a ticket to the conference?

Angular U

Call for Speakers
Submissions Close March 31, 2015

We’d like to extend an invitation to you to submit session abstracts for the Angular U conference that will be located at the Hyatt Regency in San Francisco from June 22 - 23, 2015. Workshops will run on June 24 - 25. We’re excited to have Google’s AngularJS team giving the opening keynote and have a lot of great events planned! The Angular U event focuses on igniting conversations around Angular, ES6, TypeScript, and Web Components so we’re looking for talks on those technologies and any others that are related. Talks will range from 20 to 45 minutes.

What specific Angular U topics are we looking for?
  • Any AngularJS topics (directives, data binding, factories/services, controllers, and more)
  • Web Component frameworks (Polymer, X-Tag, etc.)
  • The TypeScript language
  • Integrating ES6 or TypeScript with Angular
  • ES6 features
  • Testing Concepts
  • Structuring and Organizing Angular, ES6, or TypeScript Code
  • Development tools (Bower, Grunt, Gulp, etc.) related to Angular, ES6, TypeScript, and Web Components
  • Anything else you can think of that’s related to Angular, ES6, TypeScript, and Web Components!
Please submit multiple abstracts as this will improve your chances. Feel free to suggest an all-day conference workshop as well. Workshops will run June 24 - 25, 2015 at the same location.

What’s in it for you as a speaker?
  • The adoration of attendees
  • Free admission to the conference
  • Sponsorship or workshop profit sharing may be available

* If you need expense reimbursement in order to come, please add that information into the submission form so we know not to approve your session unless we also agree to pay expenses.

Angular 2 Roadmap Update


The Angular team at Google (lead by Brad, Igor and Misko) have been hard at work bring out the new version of AngularJS known as Angular 2. In this session these team leaders bring us up to date on the latest changes and future plans. It will also include an extended Q&A period.
